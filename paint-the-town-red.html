Paint the Town Red

02.2024

A multimedia campaign addressing period poverty through a series of three posters and an evocative short film. Utilizing a vibrant pink and red color palette paired with a juxtaposition of feminine and assertive typography, the campaign aims to captivate and inspire action. The posters combine diverse painting techniques to visually express the multifaceted impacts of period poverty, serving as both an educational narrative and a rallying cry for menstrual equity and systemic change.
