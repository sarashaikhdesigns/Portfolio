Blue Narrative

11.2024

The Blue Narrative is a gallery exhibition celebrating the revival of cyanotype photography, showcasing contemporary artists who explore this historic medium. The exhibition highlights the beauty of cyanotype while encouraging its resurgence, blending tradition with innovation.

Accompanying the exhibition is a 16-page saddle-stitched book that invites personal reflection on belonging and identity. Combining risograph printing, inkjet printing, and laser cutting, the book merges tactile craftsmanship with meaningful storytelling, leaving a lasting impact on its audience.

Mockup of gallery opening where guests are immersed in the world of cyanotype photography. The space is designed with blue tones, highlighting the beauty of this historic process. Alongside the exhibition, a cyanotype workshop invites attendees to create their own prints, exploring the techniques and stories behind the art.

To extend the experience, take-home cyanotype kits allow visitors to create their own prints at home. Each kit includes everything needed and instructions, making it easy for participants to continue their cyanotype journey after the event.
