WILDCARD

07.2024 - present

A creative initiative founded to transform discarded fabric and materials into one-of-a-kind designs. WILDCARD reflects a commitment to environmental responsibility and artistic expression, blending sustainability with personal storytelling. Every creation celebrates the journey of its wearer, serving as both a statement of style and a symbol of positive impact. More than a brand, WILDCARD is a movement towards a sustainable and artistic future in fashion. I handle all aspects of creative direction, photography, website, and social media management to bring this vision to life.
