Heart Rot

01.2024

A collaborative 8-page zine created for the NYC indie rock band Heart Rot, known for their 1990s-inspired quiet-loud dynamics and emotionally textured sound. The zine, which unfolds into a poster, reflects the band’s distinct identity and was distributed at live performances. Additionally, a custom logo featuring interlocking heart, star, and spiral shapes was designed to symbolize the unity and creativity of the band’s three women members.
